# MoodMusic Privacy Policy

## Data Collection and Use

### Spotify Data
- **What we collect**: We only access your Spotify account data necessary for music playback and playlist management
- **Required permissions**: 
  - Read your playback state
  - Modify your playback state
  - Read your playlists
  - Read your email (for account identification)
- **Data retention**: We do not store your personal Spotify data long-term. All data is fetched on-demand and not cached beyond the current session.

### User Feedback
- **Mood preferences**: Stored locally in your browser session
- **Genre preferences**: Used only for playlist adaptation
- **No personal data**: We do not collect personal information beyond what's necessary for the app to function

### Third-Party Services
- **Spotify**: We use Spotify's official Web API with proper OAuth authentication
- **No data sharing**: We do not share your data with any third parties except Spotify (as required for music playback)

## Compliance
- Follows Spotify Developer Terms and Policy
- Implements proper OAuth scopes with minimal required permissions
- No content training or ML model ingestion
- Respects Spotify's branding and attribution requirements

## Your Rights
- You can revoke Spotify access at any time through your Spotify account settings
- All data is processed locally in your browser
- No account creation required - uses your existing Spotify account

## Contact
For privacy concerns, please contact us through the app interface.
